GOING DEEPER

14 - 24 MAY 2020
Cost: €950

This fiction writing retreat is perfect for those who are interested in writing a publishable novel and have already written several short-stories. You will gain a deep understanding and knowledge of story, and you will leave the retreat motivated to start or finish your book.

We will be focusing on accessing and deepening imaginative concepts, using our life experience , strengthening writing voice, shape characters. You will learn how to improve your form setting, frame theme, and create structure.

You will be encouraged to explore story in a genre of your choice, be it Literary Fiction, Science Fiction/Fantasy, Crime, Romance, Children’s, …or to discover and find your genre as you progress through the retreat.


Retreat includes:
  • *Fiction Workshop with Anne Shirley
  • *Guided Walks in the near-by forest
  • *Special Tour of the lake Island – Secret Beach!
  • *Private Bedroom en suite, per participant (no supplement fees)
  • *Home Cooked Breakfast each day
  • *3 x Lunches
  • *2 x Dinners
  • *Writing Journal
