Our contact details and how to find us:

The retreat centre is located on the eastern shore of the Lake of the Shining Waters in northern Italy, only 40 km from Verona. The centre is well accessible by car even in the winter time. We are secluded from the main tourist spots and have access to a private beach with possibility of swimming in the pristine waters of the lake.
We offer pick-up service from a nearby train station.

Address: Kindred Spirits Retreat Centre,
Lungolago Garibaldi 3,
Peschiera del Garda, VR

Email: info@kindredspirits.com
Phone: 09 760 342

GPS: 41 24.2028, 2 10.4418.
The nearest train station is in Bardolino, only 5 km from the retreat centre. If you travel by train, please contact us one day prior to your arrival and we will arrange to pick you up.
If you travel by car, take Exit 28 from N7 in the direction of Bardolino. You can also use our GPS details: 41 24.2028, 2 10.4418.
Verona International Airport is only 40 km away from the retreat centre. Take a train to Bardolino from the Stazione Centrale di Verona and we will pick you up at the train station. Please let us know 24 hours before your arrival that you require a pick-up.
